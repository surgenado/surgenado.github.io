#SurgeThursday

Instructions

1. Pick a time that you normally drive but will log off when the #Surgenado goes live.

2. Click the button to add it to your calendar

3. When the reminder pops up, follow the link to see if #SurgeThursday is a GO or a NO GO

4. Recruit! A minimum viable number of drivers is required

5. Once that number is reached, drivers syncronize logouts

6. And a #Surgenado is generated

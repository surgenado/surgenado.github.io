What is a Surgenado?

Just like The Wave in a stadium, a Surgenado is a rolling surge generated by coordinating driver logouts.

Unlike A Strike, drivers continue to earn while causing a disruption to the target TNC's business operations

Participation is simple and anonymous

1. Drivers anonymously download a schedule of their choosing.
2. Drivers anonymously check in weekly on their schedule.
When a critical mass of driver checkins is achieved...
3. Drivers coordinated logouts collectively generate a Surgenado.
4. Which all drivers enjoy the benefits of.

#Surgenado

What is The Surgenado?

Like The Wave in a stadium, The Surgenado is a rolling surge wave generated by coordinated driver logouts.

Why do The Surgenado?

Unlike A Strike, The Surgenado allows drivers to protest working conditions by disrupting the target TNC's business operations while continuing to earn a living.

Participation is anonymous and simple

1. Drivers download a schedule of their choosing.
2. Drivers anonymously check in on their schedule.
When a critical mass of drivers have checked in...
3. Coordinated sequential driver logouts collectively combine to generate A Surgenado.
